Nearby Places

Dhamma Giri

Dhamma Giri means ‘Hill of Dhamma.’ One of the world’s largest meditation centers and the main center of a rapidly growing global Vipassana organization - Dhamma Giri dedicated to the teaching of Vipassana Meditation.

Tringalwadi Fort

Situated at an elevation of 3000 feet, Tringalwadi Fort is believed to have been constructed in the 10th century. Hiking and trekking enthusiasts often make their way here to reach to the top of the fort that looks like a turban and is one of the popular places to visit in Igatpuri.

Camel Valley

A perfect spot for nature lovers and photographers, Camel Valley lies a few kilometers ahead of Bhatsa river valley. It is a valley worth visiting during the monsoons as it opens out to waterfalls and offers splendid scenic views.

Amruteshwar Temple

Amruteshwar Temple is an ancient Shiva temple in the Igatpuri region. The ceiling and the walls of the temple are decorated with some impressive and lovely mural, while the pillars have beautifully carved statues and flowers.

Karoli Ghat

A drive to Karoli ghat can be a beautiful experience for travel and nature enthusiasts; especially with the rains pouring. The trek is a medium to difficult level one and requires high endurance level. It easily takes about 2 hours to complete the trail and reach the peak.
